About Us

We are a bunch of experienced silicon valley entrepreneurs, energetic engineers, and research scientists with 20+ years of experience in building products/platforms in FinTech, RegTech and AI/ML.

The products we have built/building are venture funded and backed by giants in the industries. Some of the areas we are working right now includes blockchain, crypto, explainable AI/ML, and search engines.

The platforms we built are solving high risk banking problems in cannabis industries and explaining the hidden biases in the heart of the ML systems at Fintech companies.

The CXO's of the company are from companies like Microsoft, and Element AI. We have filed patents in AI/ML systems and search technologies.

Partners

PointChain is a blockchain based banking ecosystem built to solve high risk cannabis businesses financial problems, as part of that we provide features like B2B and B2C transactions, smart contracts, capital lending for cannabis businesses, high volume payment APIs for internet payments and programs like BNPL.

Armilla AI is a Quality Assurance platform for models allowing large enterprises to governance, deploy and scale their Machine Learning systems. Our clients leverage Armilla AI to govern, validate, test, and monitor any and all models across the enterprise in a reliable, consistent, and repeatable manner.

DDIQ is a risk-based cognitive computing platform that combines automation with the skills of a human researcher to uncover and analyze regulatory risks of a subject that are not found using current techniques. DDIQ empowers compliance teams at financial institutions, corporations, and investment firms to mitigate risk and meet the increasing demands of regulators to automatically and constantly screen all available public data about a subject while preparing auditable reports.
